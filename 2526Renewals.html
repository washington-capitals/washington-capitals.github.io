PAYMENT OPTIONS

Pay In Full

Pay your account in full by March 17, 2025 via credit card or check payable to Washington Capitals.

50-50

Pay 50% of your total invoice upon renewal and your same credit card will be automatically charged the remaining 50% of your invoice on August 15, 2025.

4-Part Payment

Spread out over four payments, paying 25% of your invoice upon renewal and subsequent 25% payments made on or around June 15, 2025, August 15, 2025, and October 15, 2025.

Auto-renew Power Pay Monthly Payment Plan*

12 equal installment payments made via credit card, first payment made at the time of renewal, subsequent payments made on or around the 15th of each month through February 15, 2026.

*NOTE FOR POWER PAY MEMBERS

If you choose to enroll in 12-month auto renew Power Pay, your Club Red 365 Membership will automatically renew for future seasons on our 12-month payment plan.

Under the 12-month payment plan, you will be charged the effective membership price beginning on the stated renewal deadline, and on or around the 15th of each subsequent month for the duration of each season's 12-month payment cycle (March through February), on the payment credit card you provide.

The effective membership price, which will be based on the membership pricing in effect at the time of renewal, will be communicated to you each eyar before the first payment in the new season's cycle.

The effective membership price is provided in the season ticket membership price chart, which is located on the capitals membership renewal website each season.

You may change from the 12-month auto renew payment plan to another of the offered payment plans at any time by contacting your membership services manager via phone or email; provided, that you must contact your membership services manager before the 15th of any month to avoid being charged for the next month on the monthly plan.

Members enrolled in 12-month Power Pay are automatically enrolled in Pay As We Play payment program for Playoffs.
